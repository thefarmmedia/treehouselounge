First time here? Start here.

Most people are nervous. You won't be by step three.

Here's exactly what happens from the parking lot to your first conversation on the couch — no surprises.

01
2 minutes

Arrive and check in

Pull into Summers of the River Sports Complex in Nixa. Walk up to the front desk — someone will greet you right away. Bring a valid government ID; you must be 21 or older, no exceptions.

Tip: Friday and Saturday after 8pm get busy. If you want a quieter first visit, come between 2–5pm.

02
3 minutes

Sign the waiver

A quick safety waiver — standard for any consumption space. You can sign it on a tablet at the door, or fill it out online ahead of time so check-in takes thirty seconds.

Ask the concierge what's in the waiver
03
BYOC

Bring your own cannabis

The Treehouse doesn't sell product — you bring whatever you're comfortable with. Don't have anything yet? Show up anyway. This community shares generously, and partner brands regularly drop off samples for members to try.

Need gear? Rent a bowl, gravity bong, or vaporizer (including a Volcano or Puffco Peak Pro) for $5–20/day — cleaned thoroughly between uses.

04
Social

Meet the community

Sink into the leather couches in the living room area, or pull up to a table. Staff and regulars are quick to wave new faces over — most first-timers say they were talking with someone within ten minutes.

05
Stay as long as you like

Enjoy games, events, and the lounges

Switch, PS5, Oculus Quest, darts, pool, foosball, big-screen TVs — or just see what's on tonight's calendar: live music, workshops, art classes, yoga, bingo, open mic. Check tonight's lineup before you go.
